Here we have some templates for FirewallBuilder



-	SimpleServerSCIFI
	  - no vlans
	  - with everything
	  - two interfaces
	    - eth0 - external network
	    - eth1 - intenal network
	  - up to 62 APs
	  - up to 126 simultaneous users
	  - create a second machine for contingence is a good idea

-	CentralServerSCIFI
	  - with vlans
	  - with 4 interfaces
	    - eth0 - external
	    - eth1 - users
	    - eth2 - network devices
	    - eth3 - visitors

- 	AreaServer
	  - without vlans
	  - with e interfaces
	    - eth0 - external
	    - eth1 - users
